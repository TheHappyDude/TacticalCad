Firestrike Brass Barrel Adapter



Info

This is a drop-in replacement barrel designed to friction fit a 17/32" brass barrel, without having to destroy the original one. It is designed directly off the original barrel, with only slight modifications to make the part stronger. It is compatible with every other part on the Firestrike.

NOTE: Be careful with the tabs, since they are easy to break due to the nature of 3D printing compared to injection molding.
