Firestrike Plunger Coupler



Info

This is a drop-in replacement assembly for removing the air restrictor so you can easily put it back without destroying it if needed. It allows a clear airflow from the plunger to the barrel.

NOTE: This part was designed to be looser than it's supposed to so you can put in teflon tape or electrical tape to get a better seal.
